Coffee

Description

Coffee is delicacy in many parts of the world.A delicious beverage that can be tailored to individual interests and is often the first thing many people drink in the morning.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Coffee beans or Coffee powder
  • Hot Water
  • Other ingredients to your liking

Steps

  1. Boil the milk
  2. Boil the water
  3. Add some milk to water(amount of milk can be adjusted according to personal preference)
  4. Add coffee powder(if you have coffee beans instead, make powder form the beans)
